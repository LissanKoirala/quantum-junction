Each panel below shows the best distribution evidence retained for one challenge. Exact panels show the largest exact probabilities from statevector simulation. Sampled panels show the top observed bitstrings from Quimb MPS, MPO-unswap, or MPS distillation evidence; the grey tail is all unplotted probability or sample mass. Green bars mark a plotted bitstring that equals the selected candidate. Orange panels mean no answer was selected even though a low-confidence distribution was available. Bitstrings in plots are shortened for readability; the full selected bitstrings remain in the selected candidate table above. All bitstrings use Qiskit count order.
